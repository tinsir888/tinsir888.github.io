Existence

🤔Core-Stability exist in FL if:

  1. Utility function is continuous.

  2. The conical combination of utility functions is convex.

    ∀⟨α1,⋯ ,αn⟩∈R≥0n\forall\langle\alpha_1,\cdots,\alpha_n\rangle\in\mathbb R_{\ge0}^n∀⟨α1​,⋯,αn​⟩∈R≥0n​, the set C={θ∣∑i∈[n]αiui(θ)}C=\{\theta|\sum_{i\in[n]}\alpha_iu_i(\theta)\}C={θ∣∑i∈[n]​αi​ui​(θ)} is convex.
