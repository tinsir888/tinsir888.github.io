Familiy of Envy-freeness

Envy-freeness

AAA is EF if ∀i,j∈N\forall i,j\in N∀i,j∈N, vi(Ai)≥vi(Aj)v_i(A_i)\ge v_i(A_j)vi​(Ai​)≥vi​(Aj​).

Deciding whether an instance admits EF is NP-complete.

EF is not always exist.

EF1 and Pareto Optimality

MNW allocation: an allocation AAA is MNW if

  1. It maximizes number of agents with positive value
  2. It maximizes the Nash welfare (the product of valuation) for agents with positive value.

MNW→\rightarrow→EF1+PO

Familiy of Proportionality

Proportionality

AAA is PROP if ∀i∈N\forall i\in N∀i∈N, vi(Ai)≥vi(M)nv_i(A_i)\ge\frac{v_i(M)}{n}vi​(Ai​)≥nvi​(M)​.

Deciding whether an instance admits PROP is also NP-complete.

Maximin Share Fairness

MMS, a relaxation of PROP, is trying to give each agent iii bundles of value at least as her maximin share μin(M)\mu_i^n(M)μin​(M).

Maximin share is the worst value agent can guarantee for herself by partitioning MMM into nnn disjoint bundles and keeping the worst of them.

Let An(M)\mathcal A_n(M)An​(M) be te collection of possible allocations of the goods in MMM to nnn agents.

AAA is MMS if ∀i∈N\forall i\in N∀i∈N:

vi(Ai)≥μin(M)=max⁡B∈An(M)min⁡S∈Bvi(S)v_i(A_i)\ge\mu_i^n(M)=\max_{B\in\mathcal A_n(M)}\min_{S\in B}v_i(S)vi​(Ai​)≥μin​(M)=B∈An​(M)max​S∈Bmin​vi​(S)

MMS don’t always exist when there are more than 2 agents.
